Persona Strategy
The Audience Truth
Both audiences are deeply skeptical of being marketed to. Same product, same message, completely different language.
💻
The Developer
Skeptical. Technical. Marketing-averse.
Primary Pain
AI-generated code they can't fully trust, and no reliable framework to verify it at scale.
What Resonates
Guide → Generate → Verify → Solve. A workflow that gives them control and accountability back.
"Guide your agents. Verify their output. Fix what breaks. Repeat."
📊 96% of developers don't fully trust AI output. Only 48% always verify before committing.
Source: Sonar 2026 State of Code Developer Survey
👔
The C-Suite / Eng. Leader
Risk-focused. Outcome-driven. Vendor-fatigued.
Primary Pain
Business risk from AI-accelerated development they can't see or audit, at 10x the volume.
What Resonates
44% reduction in AI-caused outages. Verification as governance and compliance, not just dev tooling.
"AI moves fast. Unverified code moves faster. Sonar customers are 44% less likely to experience an AI-caused outage."

Developer Communities
Where Developers Actually Live
These channels don't follow the same rules. You don't publish to them, you participate in them. The content is written, peer-to-peer, and data-first. Brand stays in the background until trust is earned.
Reddit
Top of Funnel · Awareness
Works
Data-led posts written in an engineer's voice. AMA with a Sonar engineer. r/programming, r/devops, r/ExperiencedDevs.
Kills you
Brand logos in the headline. CTAs in the body copy. Anything that reads like a press release.
"We analyzed 750B lines of AI-generated code — here's where it consistently breaks down."
Hacker News
Top of Funnel · Awareness
Works
Show HN post framed as a technical idea inviting genuine critique. Written by a Sonar engineer, not a marketer.
Kills you
Marketing language. Spin. Anything that doesn't invite pushback. The HN audience will destroy it.
"Show HN: A framework for verifying AI-generated code at scale — Guide, Generate, Verify, Solve."
Dev.to
Top → Mid Funnel · SEO Long-shelf
Works
Long-form peer-to-peer essays. "The Death of CI" lives here. SEO-friendly, longer shelf life than Reddit or HN.
Kills you
Repurposed blog posts that weren't written for a developer audience. Generic content with no POV.
"The era of continuous integration is rapidly coming to an end. Here's what replaces it."
Discord
Top of Funnel · Underutilized Channel
Works
Organic presence in developer-focused servers over time. Where younger devs live. Very few B2B companies doing this well yet.
Kills you
Joining a server, posting a link, and disappearing. Community channels require sustained participation to earn trust.
Sonar's existing open-source community is the seed. Grow from there — don't cold-enter.

Measurement Framework
How We Measure It
Reach metrics and conversion metrics optimize for different things. Conflating them is how content strategies fail. We check both at 30 days and 90 days.
Reach Metrics
Awareness track
Video view-through rate
YouTube Shorts + LinkedIn clips — are people watching past 10 seconds?
Community post engagement rate
Reddit upvotes, HN points, Dev.to reactions and comments — quality signals over volume.
LinkedIn save and share rate
Save rate is the strongest intent signal on LinkedIn — stronger than likes.
Newsletter open and click-through rate
Benchmark: 35%+ open rate for developer-focused B2B newsletters.
Conversion Metrics
Conversion track
Email capture rate from gated content
State of Code Report and AC/DC Whitepaper — what % of visitors convert to leads?
Demo requests attributed to AC/DC content
UTM-tagged links from every asset. This is the number that matters to sales.
SonarQube trial signups from content traffic
Developer-track conversion. Content-influenced pipeline, not just direct.
Pipeline influenced by AC/DC campaign
The full-funnel number. Multi-touch attribution from first touch to closed opportunity.
30-Day Checkpoint
Catch what isn't working early. Optimize top-of-funnel channels based on engagement data. Adjust community content if Reddit/HN posts aren't landing.
90-Day Checkpoint
Evaluate full-quarter campaign. Attribute pipeline to content. Decide which assets to double down on for Q2.
AI-Augmented Workflow
The AI-Augmented Workflow
AI handles velocity. Human judgment protects the Sonar voice. Every tool has a specific job and a clear stopping point where craft takes over.
Ds
Descript
Transcription + Clip Extraction
Upload the keynote video. Auto-transcribe. Identify the strongest 30-60 second moments using word emphasis and scene detection. Export clips in 16:9, 9:16, and 1:1 simultaneously. Get a first draft of social copy pulled directly from the transcript.
Cl
Claude
Ideation + Long-form Writing
Write the AC/DC Playbook newsletter series. Refine and pressure-test social copy from Descript. Draft the Reddit and Hacker News posts in a peer-to-peer voice. Prompt with persona, funnel stage, and tone specified explicitly — output is reviewed against the Sonar voice before anything ships.
Rw
Runway
Image + Video Creation
Generate social graphics like the 44% stat card and the AC/DC infographic visual. Create simple animations and motion graphics for video content. Brand colors, font, and layout are locked to Sonar's visual identity before generation begins so output stays on-brand.
EL
ElevenLabs
AI Voice Cloning
When video content requires narration or a consistent voice across assets, ElevenLabs enables AI voice cloning for scalable production. Used only with full consent from the voice owner. Particularly useful for creating localized or accessible versions of content without scheduling additional recording sessions.
